About Us

Navraj Global Advisors (NGA) is a boutique tax and advisory firm specializing in resolving complex cross-border challenges for businesses, entrepreneurs, and global families.

We bring clarity to ambiguity—navigating regulatory frameworks across jurisdictions with precision, foresight, and integrity.

In India, NGA delivers tax and regulatory advisory across direct taxes, litigation, and corporate compliance. In UAE, we focus on taxation advisory aligned with evolving federal and emirate-level laws. In USA, our services span taxation and regulatory advisory, ensuring clients meet both IRS requirements and state-level obligations.

Founded by Nalini Mathur along with a team of seasoned Chartered Accountants, NGA draws on over two decades of hands-on experience in litigation, International taxation, Indirect taxation and Corporate advisory. We combine deep technical expertise with commercially grounded insights to deliver solutions that work in practice, not just on paper.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to empower clients to make smarter, compliant, and growth-oriented decisions—wherever they operate in the world.
